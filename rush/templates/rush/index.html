Recruitment FAQ

How do I sign up?

You can click the "Sign Up for Recruitment" button in the recruitment tab or visit our Instagram, and the form will also be provided on our other marketing material. Just scan the QR code you see with your phone's camera then click the prompt you receive.

What is an "ideal" candidate?

There is no cookie cutter definition of an "ideal" candidate. Our brothers love learning more about your passions and how you pursue them. We strive to be a tenacious and driven group of individuals, and we are always waiting to be inspired by new members who join our community.

How many brothers should I talk to at events?

We encourage you to have meaningful conversations with as many brothers as possible at each of our events. However, while the two are not mutually exclusive, quality is preferable to quantity.

How big of a time commitment is joining the fraternity?

While deciding to pursue membership in Alpha Kappa Psi may seem daunting or overwhelming, our brothers have all balanced academics and other extracurricular activities with their commitments to joining and actively participating in the fraternity. Additionally, our brothers are always happy to provide academic support in the form of tutoring.

Will recruitment events be held in person?

Per Boston University's Learn from Anywhere guidelines, recruitment events will be entirely virtual.

How will an online process be different than an in person one?

Although the virtual environment will change how we host events, we've done our best to ensure that the content delivered and interactions you have with brothers remain of the utmost quality. Recruitment events will be held on Zoom, and we will have networking opportunities through the platform as well. There will be a recruitment team to help troubleshoot any technical issues.

Will I be able to go through recruitment if I'm not in Boston this semester?

Yes! Our recruitment events are open to all Boston University students, regardless of location.
